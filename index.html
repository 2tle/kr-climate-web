The Solutions of Korea

Carbon neutral point energy system

Carbon neutral point energy system

Korea has a carbon-neutral point energy system. Through this, people can receive points according to the reduction of electricity, water supply, and city gas usage. Points are exchanged for cash, transportation costs, gift certificates, etc. The system causes people to voluntarily reduce their energy use.

Economical Transportation Card

Economical Transportation Card

In Korea, there's an 'Economical Transportation Card' backed by the government and local authorities. It helps you accumulate mileage and obtain transportation discounts for over 15 public transit rides per month. This promotes increased public transportation usage, aiding in energy conservation.

Nanjido Island

Nanjido Island

Nanjido Island is an island located in Seoul, where 92 million tons of garbage, 33 times the size of the Giza Pyramid, have been thrown away for 15 years. After the closure, a project to create an ecological park on Nanjido Island was carried out. As a result, Nanjido Island has been transformed into a beautiful green park, and the energy of methane gas and other waste mixtures is used as energy sources for nearby areas.
